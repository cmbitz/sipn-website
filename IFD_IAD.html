First Ice-Free and Ice-Advance Date

First Ice-Free Day (IFD) is the day of the year upon which the sea ice concentration first drops below 15% during a given year. First Ice-Advance Day (IAD) is the day of the year after the summer minimum upon which the ice concencration first exceeds 15% during a given year.

Dark gray indicates regions where the ice concentration was never below 15% (or never yet for 2019). It is too early to compute IAD for 2019.
