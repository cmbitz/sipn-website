June 2018 Report Figures

Figure 3. 2018 Sea Ice Outlook mean monthly September Sea Ice Probability (SIP) from 9 dynamical models and 1 statistical model, plus the mean and standard deviation across all forecasts.



Most forecasts show open conditions for most of the Siberian and Alaskan coastlines, and predict a low chance that the Northeast Passage remains ice covered this summer (mean SIP of ~30%). Ice conditions in the Beaufort, Chukchi, Latev, and East Greenland seas appear to be highly uncertain, with significant spread across different forecasts.

Forecasts of IFD tend to disagree on the timing of opening for most of the Arctic except for the Centeral Arctic towards to Canadian Islands, the Kara, Barents, and Chukchi Sea.
